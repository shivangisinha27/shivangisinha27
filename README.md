### 📂 Projects

#### [🔍 Employee Attrition Prediction](https://github.com/shivangisinha27/Employee_attrition_prediction)
> Analysed an IBM-style HR dataset to identify the strongest predictors of voluntary attrition using EDA, distribution analysis, and Cramér's V.

- Frequent travellers: **~25% attrition** vs ~8% for non-travellers
- Early-tenure employees (<1 yr): **~36% attrition** — highest risk group
- Business Travel & Department were the strongest predictors; salary hike alone showed weak association
